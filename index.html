IDK You Yet

Alexander 23

About the Song

"IDK You Yet" is a poignant exploration of longing and the human condition, capturing the essence of missing someone you've never met. Alexander 23's emotive lyrics and stripped-back production invite listeners into a deeply personal space, resonating with anyone who has ever felt a void in their heart.

About the Artist

Alexander 23 has quickly emerged as a voice for his generation, weaving together themes of love, loss, and introspection with his unique blend of pop sensibilities and raw emotional honesty.
